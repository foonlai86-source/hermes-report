Kitten Economics Episode 5: Incentives

Welcome back to Kitten Economics.

At home, Coco the kitten ignores cleanup time. Then the rule changes: every toy returned to the basket earns one tiny fish sticker. Behavior changes immediately. Incentives shape choices. Good incentives make the desired action easier and rewarding; bad incentives create shortcuts and gaming. In work and life, design incentives around outcomes you truly want. If you want consistency, reward consistency. If you want quality, reward quality, not just speed.

Quick recap:
- Concept: Incentives
- Cat-world story: simple and visual
- Human takeaway: one practical decision rule
