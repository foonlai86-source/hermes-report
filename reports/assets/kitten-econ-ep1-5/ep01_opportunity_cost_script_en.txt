Kitten Economics Episode 1: Opportunity Cost

Welcome back to Kitten Economics.

Milo the kitten has to choose between fish, playtime, and a nap. He learns that every yes quietly means no to something else. Opportunity cost is not what you paid; it is the best thing you gave up. In daily life, this explains why scrolling for one hour costs you more than time: it may cost your workout, your focus, or your sleep. Use three questions before any decision: What is my best alternative? Will it still matter in a week? Am I choosing short-term comfort or long-term value?

Quick recap:
- Concept: Opportunity Cost
- Cat-world story: simple and visual
- Human takeaway: one practical decision rule
