Kitten Economics Episode 2: Supply and Demand

Welcome back to Kitten Economics.

On Cat Street, rainbow feather toys suddenly become popular. Many kittens want them, but only a few are available. Prices rise because demand is high and supply is low. Later, two more toy shops open and supply increases. Prices soften. Supply and demand is a moving balance, not a fixed rule. You can use it when buying online: if everyone wants the same item today, expect higher prices. If supply expands or demand cools, better deals appear.

Quick recap:
- Concept: Supply and Demand
- Cat-world story: simple and visual
- Human takeaway: one practical decision rule
