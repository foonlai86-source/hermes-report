Kitten Economics Episode 4: Marginal Utility

Welcome back to Kitten Economics.

Luna the kitten loves tuna bites. The first bite feels amazing, the second is still great, and the sixth feels ordinary. This is marginal utility: each extra unit gives less additional satisfaction. The idea helps with spending. The first purchase may create real value, but repeated impulse purchases often add less joy. Before buying another similar thing, ask: will this next unit improve my life meaningfully, or am I just repeating a habit?

Quick recap:
- Concept: Marginal Utility
- Cat-world story: simple and visual
- Human takeaway: one practical decision rule
